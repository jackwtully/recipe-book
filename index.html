Salsa al Pomodoro

Soffritto

  1. Olive oil
  2. Diced onion
  3. Diced carrot
  4. A few basil leaves
  5. Some garlic cloves
  6. Salt & Pepper

Olive oil must cover bottom of the pan.

Add all ingredients together, wait for them to fry a bit.

Salsa al Pomodoro

  1. Concentrato di pomodoro
  2. Pasata
  3. Pinch of sugar
  4. Vino

Concentrato and pasata are added together, then add some sugar.

After a bit, increase the heat and wait for the salsa to bubble.

When bubbling, add the wine and wait until it cooks off.
